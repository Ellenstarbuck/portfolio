My main drive in life has been to create. I love taking an idea and finding novel and exciting ways to implement it. I have used my own initiative in the past to turn creative ideas into reality, from one-woman shows to plays, film scripts and even a live comedy fan fiction night. I was drawn to programming as another way to create, challenge and educate myself.
